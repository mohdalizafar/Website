    I'm a Computer Science student at McMaster University with hands-on experience in fullstack development, data analytics, machine learning, and cloud infrastructure. I've built web applications, backend systems, ML pipelines, and data-driven tools using Python, Java, C, C#, C++, SQL, JavaScript, React, Django, AWS, and other modern technologies.

    Through internships at Niwish and KUWAITNET, I've worked on real products in team environments, building APIs, improving workflows, and delivering practical solutions designed for users and scale.
